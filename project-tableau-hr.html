üöß Key Engineering Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1: Dynamic Salary Bands and Age Groups

Problem: Classifying employees into salary and age categories without hard-coding values.

Solution: Created calculated fields in Tableau for dynamic classification based on data ranges.

Challenge 2: Dashboard Interactivity Without Performance Degradation

Problem: Multiple filters and charts could slow dashboard responsiveness.

Solution: Optimized data source, minimized complex calculations on the dashboard, used efficient filters.

Challenge 3: Aligning Metrics Across Multi-Dimensional Analysis

Problem: Ensuring consistency when filtering by department, gender, and compensation simultaneously.

Solution: Implemented cross-filtering and synchronized field actions for coherent analysis.
